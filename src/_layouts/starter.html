EAT STARTER

This starter is nothing special. Really.
It just uses some of the most usefull and recent tools available. Some sane default – at least for me and my projects – you can change anytime you want. All I wanted for my setups was a really nice working static site generator, some nice and easy JavaScript and easy styling. Ok... and I wanted inlined CSS and JS, some default SEO fields and even some autolinking for interlinking pages that may come up in projects.
